About us

Welcome To Perlermom!

I am a stay at home mom who found an interest in perling beginning in 2016! I started for years doing it just as a hobby, but as of recently I've found an interest in selling my products! I sell all in stock items but I take customized orders as well! I sell all different types of perlers including

  • ○ Coasters
  • ○ Portraits
  • ○ Keychains
  • ○ Personal Self Portraits
  • ○ Magnets
  • ○ Canvases
  • ○ Perler Sprites
  • ○ Pendants
  • ○ Pendants

We work hard to provide a large variety of products for you, and to also provide great customer service. We welcome your suggestions and feedback!
